About the data & methodology

Rates are gathered by an automated scraper that surveys 22 Hong Kong time-deposit providers (8 virtual banks, 12 traditional banks, 2 wealth platforms), cross-referenced against three independent aggregators — StashAway HK, MoneyHero, MoneySmart HK — and Syfe's own monthly comparison sheet. Aggregator rates take precedence; direct fetches flag disagreements greater than 15 basis points.

Each row carries metadata that the headline number cannot: a minimum-deposit threshold (shown as a green tag), whether the rate is gated to new funds (shown as a red tag), and the date the value was last verified.

Where providers publish tiered tables (ICBC Asia, Public Bank, CMB Wing Lung), only the highest-rate tier is shown with its threshold annotated. Bank of Communications and Chong Hing Bank are tracked but excluded — their published rates fall below 0.30% p.a. across all tenors. Three providers (Citibank HK, Ant Bank, PAO Bank) require Chrome-rendered scrapes and are queued for the next cycle.
