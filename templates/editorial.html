Methodology

Rates are continuously collected by a Syfe-built crawler that scrapes provider pages in incognito sessions and validates each row against multiple independent endpoints. Where a direct fetch fails, the system silently falls through to a redundant source so no row is ever shown stale. Every value is timestamped and verified daily.
